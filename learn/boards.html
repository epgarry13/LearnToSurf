Boards

Surfboards come in all shapes and sizes. There are many different categories of surfboards and if you want to you can break them up into as many different categories as genres of heavy metal. However, there are four main types of surfboards: Shortboard, Fish, Gun, and Longboard.

Most shortboards are not ideal for beginners as they trade off stability and paddle speed for maneuverability.

A fish is generally under 6 feet long with a lot of volume packed into it. It is characterized by a wide nose, wide tail with a deep swallow cut into it, and a flat rocker. Best used by those transiitoning to a shortboard.

Not for the weak of heart or unskilled, a gun is a specialized surfboard designed for serious, death-defying waves.

A longboard is 8 feet and longer and is designed for ultimate stability (you can even walk it!) - ideal for beginners.
